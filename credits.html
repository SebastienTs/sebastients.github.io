LOBSTER

The Author

I am Sébastien Tosi, I work as a signal processing engineer at the Advanced Microscopy Core Microscopy Facility (ADMCF) of IRB Barcelona. This project would not have been possible without the projects and the images from the users of the facility, and the sample images that they kindly shared with me. I also greatly thank all my colleagues at the facility for the sample preparation and acquisition of some of the images, and my relatives who have seen and heard me hallucinating about LOBSTER for about the past 3 years.
